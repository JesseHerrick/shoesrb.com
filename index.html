Shoes

Shoes is a tiny GUI toolkit for writing fun, simple applications in Ruby.

{% highlight ruby %} Shoes.app(title: "Welcome to Shoes!") do para "Hello, world!" end {% endhighlight %}

What is Shoes?

Shoes is an awesome GUI framework for Ruby. It's cross platform and simple to use.

You could make...

  • A Todo App
  • A Book of Stories
  • A Calculator
  • A Writing App
  • A Gallery

Ready to start? Great.

Getting started with Shoes is easy! The walkthrough is the easiest way to get started with Shoes, but there are lots of other resources to help you along the way.
